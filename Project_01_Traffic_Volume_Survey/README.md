# Traffic Volume Survey & Road Safety Analysis

## Project Overview

This project presents a Traffic Volume Survey and Road Safety Analysis conducted at the IIT (ISM) Main Gate, Dhanbad. The study was carried out as part of a B.Tech. Civil Engineering mini project to evaluate traffic flow characteristics, identify road safety issues, and propose suitable engineering recommendations.

---

## Objectives

- Conduct a classified traffic volume survey.
- Determine hourly traffic volume and vehicle composition.
- Identify peak traffic periods.
- Assess road safety conditions.
- Recommend practical engineering improvements.

---

## Study Location

- **Location:** IIT (ISM) Main Gate, Dhanbad
- **Survey Type:** Manual Traffic Volume Survey
- **Survey Duration:** Morning and Evening Peak Hours

## Key Findings

- Two-wheelers constituted the highest proportion of traffic.
- Morning peak hour traffic volume was approximately **702 vehicles/hour**.
- Evening peak hour traffic volume was approximately **695 vehicles/hour**.
- Major issues observed included illegal parking, faded road markings, and inadequate pedestrian facilities.
